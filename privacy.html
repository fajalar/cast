Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Spell Meditation is a free, and slightly silly, tiny app.

The short version of this Privacy Policy: Spell Meditation doesn't want your data and doesn't keep any. This page explains the privacy details, including the few third parties that unavoidably see a little of your data.

What Spell Meditation collects

Nothing. There are no accounts, no logins, and no forms. Spell Meditation sets no cookies of its own and stores nothing on your device or on any server we control. The count of spells you cast lives only in your browser's memory and disappears the moment you close the tab.

Third parties that touch data

Hosting. Like nearly all websites, our host keeps standard server logs (such as your IP address and browser type) for security and to keep the site running. We don't build profiles from these.

Fonts. Spell Meditation loads a typeface from Google Fonts, so Google may receive your IP address when the page loads. Google describes its handling of that in its own privacy policy.

Tips. If you choose to chip in a dollar or two, the payment is handled entirely by Square. Any information you enter goes to Square under their privacy policy. Spell Meditation never sees or stores your card details.

Tracking

Spell Meditation does not run analytics or advertising trackers. There is no cookie banner because there are no cookies to consent to. We won’t know if zero people or millions of people used it.

Children

Spell Meditation is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them. If you are uncomfortable with the “Spell” framing, don’t use the site or let your kids use it. That’s a good compromise.

Changes

If this policy changes, the date at the top of this page will change with it.

Contact
